For this project the goal is to create two different classes.
One being Team that has member variables such as:
  Name
  Coach_Name
  Home_City
  Home_Status(bool: true or false)
  Score
  Timeout_Count
The other being scoreboard that has member variables such as:
  Being able to hold two different Team class (objects)
  Display the information from both of those in a scoreboard format.
  Make the scoreboard function and able to be updated with your input from the keyboard.
